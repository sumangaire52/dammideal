FAQ

How do I order a product?

Ordering a product is easy. Just click on the product you are interested in and you will see a page showing detail of that product. In the same page you will see a button saying Add to cart. Click on that button and you will see a page showing detail of your cart and a Order button. Click on that button and provide your information.

Why do I need to verify my phonenumber?

We need to verify the phonenumber to prevent fake orders by making sure that the person ordering the product is owner of that phonenumber.

When will I receive the products I ordered?

Typically, you should receive your products within 48 hours but this can vary due to strikes and traffic jam.

I missed my delivery what do I do now?

No need to worry. You can always contact us and ask us to deliver your product again.

Why do I need to pay the delivery charge?

When you order something we need to visit the vendor of that product first and then deliver it to you. This process can be expensive so you need to pay the delivery charge yourself.

I am a vendor. How can I sell with you?

In order to sell with us you need to fill this form first. Once we received your application we will review it and may contact you for further information. If your application is accepted we will contact you.
